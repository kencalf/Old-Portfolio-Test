Scanner Project. Basically this is the same concept/idea as the Books of change prokect idea. But this one involved us to scan in objects, textures, pictures, and items that we could import into photoshop and create our piece with. This project did not allow text. We printed the final pieces 11x17 inches. The items I scanned helped me develope my character for my piece, and also communicate my message. First I came up with several apocolyptic sketches, which turned into the current idea. I began grabbing images from google, to make a low resolution mockup of what i wanted. I then scanned in as many textures and objects as I could. I worked with these and some reference images from google, to create the final piece. I failed at trying to make the perspective in this piece realistic, so next time I will work on this.
